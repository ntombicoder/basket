CLARENS

About Clarens, South Africa.

Located at the base of the Maluti Mountains in South Africa's Free State province, the little town of Clarens is known as the "Jewel of the Eastern Free State" due to its charming location. It was founded in 1912 and given the name of the Swiss town of Clarens, where fugitive politician Paul Kruger spent his final days.

Many famous painters call Clarens home or visit often because of the abundance of galleries that line its main plaza and pepper the rest of town. Clarens is a favorite escape for city dwellers from Johannesburg, Bloemfontein, and Durban due to its peaceful village atmosphere, beautiful scenery, and warm climate. Clarens is one of the most beautiful places in South Africa, and its sandstone mountains and pleasant environment have earned it reputation as some of the country's top Trout Fishing grounds.

Clarens Triva!

  • All around Clarens are stunning mountain ranges, including the Maloti Mountains, the Rooiberge, and the Golden Gate Highlands National Park, all of which can be seen from the city.
  • There are various art galleries in Clarens, all of which showcase the talents of local painters.
  • Hiking, rock climbing, horseback riding, mountain biking, and whitewater rafting are just some of the outdoor adventure activities that thrive in Clarens.
  • Several brewers and cideries use local ingredients to produce handcrafted beer and cider for visitors to enjoy in Clarens.
  • In Clarens, you may choose from a number of restaurants serving authentic regional specialties including South African braai(barbeque)and boerewors(sausage).

Famous attractions.

Titanic Rock

The Titanic Rock is a big, magnificent rock formation in the Free State area of South Africa. It is near the town of Clarens. It got its name because the pointy part that sticks out of the ground makes it look like the RMS Titanic, which sank. The rock is made of sandstone, which gives it a unique reddish-brown color. It is thought to be about 190 million years old. The Titanic Rock is a popular place for tourists to visit, and people who come to the area often take pictures of it. It is also a famous place for rock climbing and hiking, and from the top you can see a beautiful view of the area.

The gallery's mission is to exhibit and promote revolutionary artwork by contemporary artists from South Africa and beyond.The Smudge Contemporary Art Gallery is a bright, airy, and contemporary venue for displaying artwork. Visitors will be able to get a close-up and personal view of the artworks on show simply due to the gallery's creative layout.The gallery does more than just display artwork; they also provide services including art consultation, framing, and installation. Staff members are enthusiastic about art and want to share their knowledge with visitors.

Clarens Brewery

Among the many attractions of the picturesque South African town of Clarens is the famed Clarens Brewery. The brewery takes great pleasure in its ability to satisfy a wide variety of palates with its handcrafted brews, which it claims are made with only the freshest ingredients. The brewery captures the small-town character of Clarens with its rustic and welcoming atmosphere.Guests can try the beers on tap or take home bottles or cans to enjoy later. Visitors to the brewery can take part in guided tours that cover every step of the brewing and bottling processes.

Golden Gate Highlands National Park

Golden Gate Highlands National Park is a protected place in South Africa. It is in the province of Free State, close to the town of Clarens. The park is called Golden Gate because of the golden sandstone rocks and outcrops that can be found there.Visitors of all ages and interests flock there to take in the breathtaking natural beauty, rich wildlife, and fun outdoor pursuits.
